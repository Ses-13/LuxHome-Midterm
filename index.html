About Us

Redefining Smart Living

LuxHome is a distinguished smart home integration service operating under NXGEN.INC, dedicated to delivering seamless, secure, and sophisticated smart home solutions for discerning residential clients.

Founded in 2025, we identified a crucial market need where homeowners desired the benefits of smart technology without the complexity of technical implementation. Our team of experts bridges this gap, transforming houses into intelligently connected homes.

We specialize in curating compatible smart devices and integrating them into a unified ecosystem, manageable through a single, intuitive platform. Our commitment to excellence ensures every installation reflects the luxury and precision our clients expect.
